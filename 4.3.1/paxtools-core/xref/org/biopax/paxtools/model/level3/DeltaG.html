4   /**
5    * Standard transformed Gibbs energy change for a reaction written in terms of biochemical reactants
6    * (sums of species), delta-G'<sup>o</sup>.
7    * 
8    * delta-G'<sup>o</sup> = -RT lnK' and delta-G'<sup>o</sup> = delta-H'<sup>o</sup> - T
9    * delta-S'<sup>o</sup>
10   * 
11   * delta-G'<sup>o</sup> has units of kJ/mol.  Like K', it is a function of temperature (T), ionic
12   * strength (I), pH, and pMg (pMg = -log<sub>10</sub>[Mg<sup>2+</sup>]). Therefore, these quantities
13   * must be specified, and values for DELTA-G for biochemical reactions are represented as 5-tuples
14   * of the form (delta-G'<sup>o</sup> T I pH pMg).
15   * 
16   * This property may have multiple values, representing different measurements for
17   * delta-G'<sup>o</sup> obtained under the different experimental conditions listed in the 5-tuple.
18   */
19  public interface DeltaG extends ChemicalConstant
